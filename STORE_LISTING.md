## Detailed Description (for store listing page)

**Tired of manually admitting people to your Google Meet?**

Auto Admit for Google Meet automatically lets participants into your meetings the moment they knock. No more interruptions, no more making people wait – just seamless meetings.

### ✨ Key Features

**🚀 Automatic Admission**
- Instantly admits participants waiting in the meeting lobby
- Works continuously throughout your meeting
- No manual clicking required

**🎯 Simple Control**
- Easy on/off toggle from the toolbar
- Visual status indicator shows when active
- Works only when you want it to

**🔒 Privacy-Focused**
- No data collection
- No tracking
- No external servers
- Open source code

**⚡ Fast & Reliable**
- Real-time monitoring using advanced DOM observation
- Backup polling every second to never miss anyone
- Lightweight and efficient

### 📋 How It Works

1. Install the extension from Chrome Web Store
2. Join or host a Google Meet as usual
3. The extension automatically admits anyone who knocks
4. Toggle it off anytime from the toolbar if needed

Perfect for:
- Educators hosting virtual classes
- Business professionals running meetings
- Team leaders conducting standups
- Anyone who frequently hosts Google Meet calls

### 🛡️ Permissions Explained

The extension only requests essential permissions:
- **activeTab**: To interact with Google Meet
- **storage**: To remember your on/off preference
- **meet.google.com**: To work only on Google Meet pages

### 💡 Important Notes

- You must be the meeting host or have admission permissions
- Works on meet.google.com only
- Does not work on external Google Meet links in some enterprise setups
- Extension must be enabled for auto-admission to work
